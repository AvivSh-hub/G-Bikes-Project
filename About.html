About Us

"G-Bikes" hit the road as a chain of shops in the year 1981 Both founders were wind surf champions, which started windsurfers shops separately. Since their merger, the chain of "G-Bikes" grows and today owns 32 shops with 140 employees that are specialized in e-bicycles, bicycles and stunt scooters. "G-Bikes" is the biggest chain of its kind in Israel

The vision of

"G-Bikes"

"FOR ENRICHMENT OF LIFE BY SPORT AND LEISURE CULTURE" The company's facilities are located in the north of the country in a pastoral setting, in the middle of an avocado grove. Today "G-Bikes" is organized in two division. The first is a wholesale division and the second is a chain of self-owned stores. The wholesale division of "G-Bikes" has among its client's national sports ("Mega sport"), petrol station convenient stores ("Yellow"), and an electrical appliances chain of stores ("electric warehouse"), army and defense personal, and 118 private bicycle shops.

TOP BRANDS ONE SHOP

"G-Bikes" is the exclusive importer of the following bicycle brands. "ROCKY MOUNTAIN","" MERIDA", "GT", "SCHWINN", "MONGOOSE", "BMC", and "PINNARELLO". We also sell 3 private label bike branded bicycles under the name Rainbow, Vision and Bolt, in which our own designed e-bikes are produced with. In the field of windsurfing the brands we represent are "NEILPRYDE", "CABRINHA" and "JP". We import and represent in Israel well known and established brands such as Giro, Bell, Vittoria, Trelock, VDO, Chilli scooters, Blackburn, Motorex, Selle San Marco, Niterider, Jagwire, Yaban chains, Yakima and more. We also import and distribute clothes and accessories under two private owned labels, "Bolt" and "Black sheep". Our products are distributed by our own trucks and local forwarders on a daily basis throughout the country, from Kiryat Shmone in the north, to Eilat city in the south. Our 32 branches are located in Nahariya, Kiryat- Motzkin, Afula, Carmiel, Misgav, Haifa, Kastra-Haifa, Nataniya, Raanana, Rishon lezion, Ashdod Ad-Halom, Petah-tikva, Jerusalem, Modiin, Bet Sheaan, Beer Sheva, Eilat, Bilu, 4 shops in Tel Aviv, Hertzeliya. The size of our stores ranges between 100 and 600 sqm. All 118 employees of the firm are bike and sports lovers. The merchandise we sell is a part of their lifestyle. They ride, surf and test our products on a weekly basis and believe in our company's vision! "G-Bikes" is sponsoring "Team TNT", "EXTREAM" triathlon team, and some sport talents in Israel, among them some Israeli national champions in cycling.
